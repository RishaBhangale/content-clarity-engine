Data Retention Policy v3
Effective Date: January 2025
Department: Compliance

Section 1: Purpose
This policy defines the organization's requirements for retaining and disposing of data across all business units.

Section 2: Data Classification
Data is classified into three levels: Public, Internal, and Confidential. Each classification level has different retention requirements.

Section 3: Retention Periods
3.1 General Data
All customer data must be retained for a minimum of 90 days after account closure, after which it shall be permanently deleted from all systems including backups.

3.2 Financial Records
Financial records must be maintained for 7 years in compliance with SOX requirements. This includes all transaction logs, invoices, and payment records.

3.3 Employee Records
Employee personnel files shall be retained for 3 years after the date of separation from the organization.

Section 4: Data Deletion
4.1 Deletion Methods
All data deletion must use DOD 5220.22-M standard overwrite method. Simple file deletion or formatting is not acceptable for confidential data.

4.2 Deletion Schedule
Data deletion occurs on a monthly cycle. The compliance team reviews deletion candidates on the first business day of each month.

Section 5: Access Control
Access to retained data requires manager approval and must be logged in the access audit system. All access requests expire after 30 days.

Section 6: Backup Policy
Backups are performed daily and retained for 30 days. Monthly snapshots are retained for 1 year.

Section 7: Review
This policy is reviewed annually by the compliance committee. The next review is scheduled for January 2026.
